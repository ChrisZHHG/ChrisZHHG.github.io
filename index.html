FlashSlot

ICBC releases road-test slots in unpredictable bursts. Refreshing the page is a part-time job. FlashSlot watches the booking flow for me and pings my phone the second a seat opens in my window.

FlashSlot
A tiny headless watcher for ICBC's road-test booking. It logs in, scans the calendar for open slots within my allowed window, dedupes what it has already told me, and pushes a notification when something new shows up. Hours saved → attention returned.
